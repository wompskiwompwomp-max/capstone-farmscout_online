Smart Shopping Tips

Make the most of your market visit with these insider tips from experienced shoppers and vendors.

Best Shopping Hours

Visit early morning (6-8 AM) for the freshest selection, or late afternoon (4-6 PM) for potential discounts on perishables.

Peak freshness: 6-8 AM

Payment Methods

Most vendors accept cash only. Bring small bills (₱20, ₱50, ₱100) for easier transactions and exact change.

Cash preferred, small bills

Market Etiquette

Greet vendors with "Kumusta" or "Good morning." Don't touch produce unless you're buying. Bargaining is acceptable but be respectful.

Respectful bargaining welcome

Quality Checks

Look for bright colors, firm texture, and fresh smell. Ask vendors about harvest dates for vegetables and catch dates for fish.

Ask about freshness dates

Bring Your Own Bags

Eco-friendly shopping! Bring reusable bags or bayong. Some vendors offer plastic bags but bringing your own helps the environment.

Eco-friendly & practical

Seasonal Shopping

Buy seasonal produce for better prices and quality. Mangoes are best March-May, while root vegetables are abundant year-round.

Follow seasonal availability
