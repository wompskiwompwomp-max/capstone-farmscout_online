Our Mission

FarmScout Online was born from a simple belief: every Filipino family deserves access to honest, transparent pricing information when shopping at traditional markets. We bridge the gap between the warmth of community palengke culture and the power of modern technology.

Like a trusted family member who knows every vendor and every fair price, we empower both farmers and consumers with real-time market intelligence that promotes fairness, reduces uncertainty, and strengthens our local food economy.

Community-First Approach

Built by Filipinos, for Filipinos, with deep respect for traditional market culture.

Our Transparency Commitment

We believe in complete openness about how we collect, verify, and present market pricing information.

Data Collection

Prices collected daily between 6-8 AM from minimum 3 vendors per product category. Average prices calculated to ensure fairness.

Updated: Daily at 8:30 AM

Verification Process

Two-step verification by market administrators and our team. Cross-referenced with historical data to identify unusual price fluctuations.

Accuracy Rate: 95.8%

Quality Control

Weekly audits, seasonal adjustments, and community feedback integration. Continuous improvement based on user reports and market changes.

Response Time: < 2 hours
